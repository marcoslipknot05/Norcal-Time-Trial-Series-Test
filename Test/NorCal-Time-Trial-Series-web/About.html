    Mission

    To quote https://24hoursoflemons.com/:
    "Racing shouldn' just be for rich idiots. Racing should be for all idiots.

    Vision

    The NorCal Time Trial Series aspires to make the excitement of competitive
    racing events on iconic northern California racetracks available to all
    enthusiasts without the cost or risk of wheel-to-wheel racing.

    General Information

    The NorCal Time Trial Series will hold at least one event per month on a track
    such as Sonoma Raceway, Thunderhill Raceway, or WeatherTech Raceway at
    Laguna Seca. Full safety is required, and your car must pass technical inspection
    before you can compete.
